WE ARE THE STUDENT BRANCH OF IEEE GBPEC
When Startedmore_vert

When Startedclose

IEEE-GBPEC came into existence in 2014 under the aegis of ECE student body in G.B. Pant Engineering College. Since then, there has been no looking back for the Student Branch. What started as a technical body of student members eventually turned into a formal authority for organizing distinguished seminars and lecture series, workshops and hands-on projects throughout the year.

Session 2018-19more_vert

Session 2018-19close

The year 2018 has been a year of bliss and challenges for IEEE-GBPEC. Revived in the month of September 2018, IEEE-GBPEC had the challenge to reestablish it's legacy again. The student branch started with an orientation and organized its flagship event, Technovenza that registered huge success. Other events organized under IEEE-GBPEC are Guest lecture on wireless communication devices, mock placement, and other techno-cultural events.

Session 2019-20more_vert

Session 2019-20close

The year helped IEEE-GBPEC strengthen its established base and celebrate the legacy, our seniors left to us. The year started with an annual orientation for freshers, succeded by a guest lecture from Madam Prerna Gaur, chairperson, IEEE-Delhi section and several workshops on Flutter, android development and machine learning. We carry forward our much loved capsule classes that soared with time. Other events organized were Quiz, Debate competition, debugging and several guest lectures.
